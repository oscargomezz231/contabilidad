PRIME COSTS

They are direct materials and direct labor. These costs are directly related to the production

PRODUCTION COST

It is the set of expenses that are necessary to produce a service or a good.

INDIRECT COSTS

Those that are not directly attributable to the production of a certain product or service

COST OF DISTRIBUTION

All the expenses that are made to put the product on the market and obtain the recovery through the sale.

TOTAL COST OF OPERATION

Production costs (also called operating costs) are the expenses necessary to maintain a project, processing line or equipment in operation.

UNITARY COST

The unit cost is the average value that, at a certain volume of production, costs to produce one unit of the product. It is obtained by dividing the total production cost
